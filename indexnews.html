New Game modes (picture below)

We currently have 3 game modes, standard mode, easy mode and hard mode.
In update 2.1, the next major update, 3 or 4 new game modes will appear. There will be:
- Super fast mode: standard mode but with increased speed
- Sudden death mode: standard mode with a little more meteorite, at the slightest contact the player dies
- The health plus mode: hard mode but with more life for the player... but also for the monsters!
- The meteorite mode: there are no more monsters but more meteorites which are faster, dodge them or die!

The hard+ mode will arrive with a small update between 2.1 and 3.1
