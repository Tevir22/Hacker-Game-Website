August 2022 7+ Indefinite time Action 2D

Hacker game is a 2D game coded in Python3.10 using the "pygame" and "random" imports. The player embodies a hacker who can move laterally and can shoot projectiles to eliminate monsters. The monsters, of random speeds, inflict damage to the player on contact. Eliminating a monster adds 10 points to your score. Meteors of different speeds fall from the sky randomly, and kill the player if they come in contact with him. The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible in order to get the highest score possible. Beware the danger can come from all sides! Since many factors are random, no two games are the same!
Controls: click to throw / space to shoot / side arrows to move
